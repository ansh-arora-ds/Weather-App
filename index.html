User Guide

Welcome to the Weather App user guide!

1. Search: To get weather information for a specific city, type the city name into the search bar at the top or in the main temperature section and click "Search."

2. Navigation: The navigation bar has links to the "About" and "About me" pages, as well as this "User Guide."

3. Weather Info: The main page displays humidity, temperature, and wind information in three separate sections.

About This App

The Weather App is designed to provide real-time weather information, including temperature, humidity, and wind conditions, for any city you search for.

It was built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, leveraging the WeatherAPI to fetch accurate and up-to-date data.
